Waffle

UI Design Intern, December 2019

The diversification of food delivery applications, mobile, and card payments managed by different payment gateways had left cashiers to use different consoles to process payment. Moreover, the POS systems widely used in Singapore are clunky and costly, require manual backups, and hardly have optimized UI. The Waffle startup aims to modernize the POS systems into an integrated system that provides a great user experience for cashiers and managers, by integrating various payment gateways, sales data analytics, and flexible menu updates, to an iOS application.

My internship here let me develop my UI design skills by translating client requirements into the application layouts, in quick and successive iterations.
